## üü¢ Beginner Soldering Kit (Budget-Friendly)

**Audience:** brand-new makers, students, hobbyists starting with through-hole components and simple PCB work.

### Core Tools

- Entry-level **temperature-controlled soldering iron**
- Brass wool/coil **tip cleaner**
- Small **silicone work mat**
- Leaded solder, **63/37 or 60/40**, ~0.6‚Äì0.8 mm
- Basic **helping hands** (optional but nice)
- **Flush cutters**
- **Needle-nose pliers**
- Manual **solder sucker** (spring-loaded)
- **Solder wick** (~2 mm)
- ESD-safe **tweezers** (starter grade)

### Consumables

- **Isopropyl alcohol** (70‚Äì99%) + lint-free wipes
- **No-clean flux pen**
- Small **tip tinner** (optional)

### Safety

- USB or budget **fume extractor**
- **Safety glasses**

> Gets someone productive immediately with simple boards and repair without a big cash burn.

## üü° Intermediate Soldering Kit (Everyday Maker / Repair Tech)

**Audience:** folks who‚Äôve done a few boards and want cleaner joints, light microsoldering, and reliable daily tooling.

### Core Tools

- Mid-range **soldering station** (Hakko/Quick/KSGER/Pinecil V2-level)
- Swappable tips: **fine conical, chisel, bevel**
- Quality **brass coil** + **tip tinner**
- Larger **silicone work mat** with compartments
- Premium **flush cutters**
- High-quality **ESD tweezers** set
- Precision **side cutters / micro shears**
- Strong **desoldering pump** (with Teflon tip)
- Bench **fume extractor** with carbon filter

### Consumables

- High-quality leaded solder (e.g., **Kester 44** or similar)
- **Rosin flux paste** or pro-grade flux pens
- **Flux remover spray**
- **Low-temp solder paste** (optional)
- Isopropyl alcohol, **99%**
- Solder wick in multiple widths (1.5 / 2.5 / 3.5 mm)

### Add-ons

- **PCB holder / vise**
- **Magnifying lamp** or entry-level **USB microscope**
- **Kapton tape** for localized heat shielding

## üîµ Professional Soldering Kit (Rework Technician / EE Lab)

**Audience:** microsoldering techs, serious repair benches, lab setups.

### Core Tools

- High-end **soldering station** (JBC, Metcal, high-end Hakko)
- Multiple premium tips: **knife, hoof, high thermal mass** for ground planes
- **Hot air rework station** (Quick 861DW class or equivalent)
- **Preheater plate** for multilayer boards (optional but highly recommended)
- **Microscope** (boom stand, 0.5√ó Barlow, ring light) or higher-end digital scope
- **Fume extraction** with HEPA + carbon filters
- **Rotational PCB vise** or rework jig
- **Thermal tweezers** (for SMD resistor/cap clusters)

### Consumables

- Premium solder alloys (63/37 + lead-free options)
- High-grade **no-clean and water-soluble flux**
- **Flux remover** and ESD-safe wipes
- Wide range of **wick sizes and solder diameters**
- **Kapton tape**, **thermal pads**, thermal putty
- **Probe needles**, micro jump wires, and magnet wire

### Measurement & Testing

- Adjustable **bench power supply** (CC/CV)
- Solid **multimeter** (Fluke or equivalent)
- **Logic analyzer** (optional)
- **Thermal camera** (optional but powerful for fault finding)
